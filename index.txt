# about

About me

Hi Everyone! I'm a curious data enthusiast and passionate about leveraging data to empower organizations in tackling significant and complex challenges , facilitating data-driven decision-making through the extraction of valuable insights from extensive raw data. Having gained prior experience in both entertainment and consulting firms, I've had the chance to collaborate, work, and lead diverse functional teams. This has enabled me to spearhead the creation and delivery of high-quality solutions and results.

Passionate about data sciences and analytics, My extensive experience has been shaped by impactful roles as a Data Scientist II and Decision Scientist at Sling TV and Mu Sigma, providing me with a broad and comprehensive skill set in the field. Throughout my career, I had the privilege of collaborating, working and navigating diverse sectors including Telecommunications, Healthcare, Information Technology, and Services, enriching my understanding of data analytics across industries.

I am currently exploring new opportunities and am open to exciting career prospects. Feel free to connect with me for potential collaborations or job opportunities. I look forward to discussing how my skills and experiences can contribute to your team.
